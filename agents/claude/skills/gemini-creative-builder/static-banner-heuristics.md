# Static Banner Heuristics (Project-Owned)

Derived from prior static-banner workflow and kept local to this repo.

Rules:
- Keep one dominant visual claim per image.
- Reserve one clean overlay-safe area for headline/CTA.
- Prefer high-contrast focal subject against simplified background.
- Avoid tiny detail noise that breaks at ad feed sizes.
- Keep human faces natural and proportional.
- Keep text instructions short in `full` mode; avoid long sentences.
- Always produce both:
  - one conversion-safe variant
  - one bolder attention variant
